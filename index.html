Meet Your Mentor

Dr. Navneet Sahota

Founder, PhD Scientist & Educator

With a background in advanced Analytical Chemistry and research on reaction mechanisms in droplets, Dr. Sahota brings Ph.D. level rigor to high school education.


Inspired by the tenacity of Marie Curie, she believes that "Nothing is to be feared, only understood." Her teaching philosophy transforms anxiety about complex sciences into curiosity and mastery.

"My goal isn't just an 'A' on a report card. It's to see a student's eyes light up when they finally understand how the world works."

Comprehensive Curriculum

Life Science Foundations

Grade 8 | Pre-High School Prep

  • Module 1: The Scientific Method
    Designing controlled experiments & data analysis.
  • Module 2: Cell Biology
    Structure, function, and the "City of the Cell".
  • Module 3: Genetics & Heredity
    Punnett squares and introduction to DNA.
  • Module 4: Ecosystems
    Energy flow, food webs, and human impact.
  • Capstone: Design Your Own Experiment.

Honors & AP Biology

Grades 9-10 | Pre-Med Track

  • Module 1: Biochemistry
    Macromolecules, Enzymes, and Energy.
  • Module 2: Molecular Genetics
    DNA Replication, Transcription, & Translation (Central Dogma).
  • Module 3: Cell Energetics
    Photosynthesis & Cellular Respiration deep dive.
  • Module 4: Evolutionary Biology
    Natural selection, phylogeny, and population genetics.
  • Labs: Gel Electrophoresis & Bacterial Transformation simulation.
